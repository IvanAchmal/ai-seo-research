## Content
This is an example of an extension domain.

Clicks increased from 5.1K to 16.5K, impressions grew from 194K to 209K, average CTR improved from 2.6% to 7.9%, and average position improved from 40.1 to 19.3 over the last three months compared to the same period last year.

I shared the results of the main domain yesterday, which has seen close to a 40x traffic increase compared to last year and now exceeds approximately $250,000 in monthly revenue.

This extension domain is focused on a specific industry or topic, with a smaller but highly targeted topical map, supported by partial and exact match domain terms.

The main domain supports this sub brand, and both are interconnected.

In industries such as real estate, SaaS, or e commerce, it is often beneficial to create extension domains based on region, platform, purpose, or product type, as they can better penetrate their own source context.

Both domains follow similar design principles and algorithmic authorship rules.
